What is Wine Plus Cellar?

Wine Plus Cellar provides access to high quality wines at the lowest prices in town.

How does Wine Plus Cellar work?

We carefully select wines to be put up into our store. All of the wines are tasted by the panel of highly professional tasters and meet our strictly standards: both high quality and the lowest price in town!

For how long is each product offered?

Wines are offered with limited quantity and would be put off once all of the stock is sold out. Time on shelf varies; some stays for several months because of its sufficient stock, while some flash sold-out in a few minutes due to its scarcity and status of highly sought-after.

How do I join?

Membership of Wine Plus Cellar is free, and you can register on our website (www.wineplus.me).
